# Dashboard Wireframe (MVP)

## Header
- Business selector
- Date range toggle: 7d / 30d / 90d
- Quick action: "View overdue leads"

## KPI Row
- Leads today
- Average response time
- Jobs won this month
- Revenue from marketing

## Main Grid
- Lead Pipeline card
  - New
  - Contacted
  - Quoted
  - Won/Lost
- Visibility card
  - Review rating/count
  - Search clicks/impressions
  - Top queries
- Competition card
  - Top local competitors
  - Rating/review gap
  - Rank trend
- Business Math card
  - Spend
  - Leads
  - Jobs won
  - Cost per job
  - ROMI

## Mobile behavior
- Single-column card stack
- Overdue leads pinned to top
- Tap-to-call from lead rows
